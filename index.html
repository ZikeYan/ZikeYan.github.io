Zike Yan (颜子轲)

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR) at Tsinghua University, working with Dr. Guyue Zhou and Prof. Yaqin Zhang. Prior to that, I obtained my Ph.D Degree from Peking University in 2023, advised by Prof. Hongbin Zha.

My research focus on advancing the construction of spatial memory in agent lifetime. I approach the problem from three aspects: structured and compositional representations with local-global hierarchies; online optimization through continual learning to enforce adaptation to view variations and scene dynamics; a paradigm shift from map-based to map-centric approaches for robot autonomy applications. See Projects and Publications below.
